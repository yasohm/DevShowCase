## vim:
** for quit vim: **  ":q" and for ignore all changes ":q!"
** for excut a shell cmd: ** ":!ls"
** for the insert mode: ** "A""i""I"  "o" "u" leave the insert "esc"
** for the visuel mode: ** "v"
** for save changes: ** "w" save & quit "wq"
** move inside the code: ** "h":left "l":right "j":down "k":up
** activate line number: ** ":set number" ":set relativenumber"
** set default settings: ** add all settings to "vi ~/.vimrc"
*cut* "y"
*paste* "p"
*delete* "d"
